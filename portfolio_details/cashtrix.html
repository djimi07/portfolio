Project information

  • Technologies: Reactjs, Laravel, Mysql, Mongodb, Redis, MQTT HiveMq

Some details about the project

An ecommerce marketplace that caters to both B2B and B2C needs. This platform is designed to help users buy and sell real estate and automotive products effortlessly. With a user-friendly interface, users can easily navigate through the platform, post ads and make purchases. One of the standout features of this marketplace is the seller center, which enables professionals to create their own stores and reach a wider audience. This feature not only provides sellers with a platform to showcase their products but also helps them grow their sales by providing them with the necessary tools and resources.
