  • Project year: 2022
  • Mode: Fullstack
  • Technologies: Laravel, Mysql

Some details about the project

A pizza order management system designed to simplify the process of ordering and delivering pizza. The platform caters to three types of users - admin, consumers, and pizzaillos. The admin has full control over the system, including managing user accounts, updating menus, and monitoring orders. Consumers can create accounts, browse menus, customize their orders, and track the delivery status. Pizzaillos, on the other hand, receive orders, confirm deliveries, and update order status.
