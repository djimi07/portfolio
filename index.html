About me

I'm self-taught full-stack developer and medical student. I have a passion for developing innovative solutions through software and constantly strive to improve my skills in the field.

In my frontend development work, I use React for its flexibility and speed, allowing me to create dynamic, responsive user interfaces that work seamlessly across different platforms. For backend development, Laravel is my framework of choice for its scalability, robustness, and ease of use. And in database management, I use both Mysql and Mongodb, depending on the specific requirements of each project.

To ensure high-quality user interfaces, I utilize Figma for design, creating visually appealing and intuitive layouts that enhance user experience

I also have experience using Python for general purpose programming and automation tasks

As a developer, I am committed to staying up-to-date with the latest technologies and best practices. I am always eager to learn new skills and adapt to new platforms to deliver the best possible solutions to my clients.
